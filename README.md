## Capital Allocation Model
- **50% Operational Capital:** Allocated to real-world business expansion and physical assets.
- **50% Market Stability (LP):** Locked into the Liquidity Pool to ensure sustainable trading and floor price support.

## Distribution Mechanism
- Investors receive $RTC units representing their proportional stake in the capital raise.
- System automatically processes capital injections and mints/distributes equity tokens via the Legion-5 Node.
